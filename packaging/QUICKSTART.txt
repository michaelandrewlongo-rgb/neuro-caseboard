NEURO-CASEBOARD  —  QUICK START (Mac)
=====================================

1) Plug this drive into your MacBook Air.

2) Open this folder and double-click:   Install Caseboard.command
      • If macOS says "unidentified developer":
           right-click the file  ->  Open  ->  Open   (one time only)
      • Wait ~10–15 minutes, until it shows:   ✅ INSTALLED
      • It needs Wi-Fi while installing.

3) On your Desktop, double-click:       Caseboard
      • It opens in your web browser like a normal website.
      • The FIRST question is slow (it downloads the AI models once). Normal.
      • Keep the small black window open while using it; close it to stop.

You can unplug the drive after step 2 — everything is copied onto the Mac.
